Push to talk

One hotkey, no wake word

Hold it, say something, let go. Works from any app, any screen, without breaking your flow.

Integrations

Already knows your apps

Queue a song, check your inbox, pull up a PR. Done in the background while you work.

Private by design

Audio never leaves your Mac

Speech becomes text on your machine. Audio is never uploaded, stored, or kept around.

Use cases

What do you say to it?

Anything you'd do with a mouse, you can just ask for out loud. Send a message, find a file, click through menus in any app. Real sentences, not robot commands.

"Reply to Mum, tell her I'll call tonight."
Peeky opens Messages, drafts the reply, and sends it when you confirm.
"Find that PDF I downloaded last week about the lease."
Peeky digs it up and opens it, no folder hunting.
"Export this as PNG at 2x."
Peeky finds the menu and clicks it for you, in apps you barely know.
